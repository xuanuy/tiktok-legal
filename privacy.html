Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-26

This application ("the App") is a personal tool used solely by its owner to prepare short vertical videos and upload them to the owner's own TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API.

What data the App uses

  • An OAuth access/refresh token for the owner's own TikTok account, used only to upload the owner's own videos to the owner's own account inbox.
  • Video files created by the owner.

What the App does NOT do

  • It does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data from any other users.
  • It does not read other users' content or post on behalf of other users.
  • It does not use tracking, advertising, or analytics SDKs.

Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the owner's own machine and are used only to call the TikTok Content Posting API. They are not transmitted to any third party.
